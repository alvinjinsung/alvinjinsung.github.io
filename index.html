Research Interests

My research goal is to advance robotics system to perceive the physical world in a way that is similar to how humans do. Specifically, I am interested in learning and reconstructing the representation of the physical world to faciliate the learning of robot skills. To this end, I am currently focusing on the following topics, but not limited to:

  • Neural 3D Reconstruction
  • Implicit Neural Representations
  • Embodied AI
  • 3D Scene Understanding
