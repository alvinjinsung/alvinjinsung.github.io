Research Interests

My research goal is to advance robotics system to perceive the physical world in a way that is similar to how humans do. Specifically, I am interested in learning representations of the physical world through uncertainty-aware perception and action, and aligning them with human expectations. To this end, I am currently focusing on the following topics, but not limited to:

  • Neural 3D Reconstruction
  • Implicit Neural Representations
  • Embodied AI
  • 3D Scene Understanding
  • Active Perception
